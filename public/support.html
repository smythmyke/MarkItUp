Frequently asked questions and how to get help.

FAQ

How do credits work?

Each generation costs 1 credit and produces up to 3 visual variations. You get 5 free credits when you sign up. Additional credits can be purchased in the app.

What image formats are supported?

PNG, JPEG, WebP, and GIF. Maximum file size is 20MB. Images larger than 4096px are automatically resized.

Is the image editor free?

Yes. All image editing tools (crop, resize, rotate, adjust, background removal) are completely free and run locally in your browser.

Can I use generated images commercially?

Yes. You retain full rights to any images you generate. See our Terms of Service for details.

My generation failed. Do I lose a credit?

If the generation fails on our end, the credit is not deducted. If partial results are returned (e.g., 1 of 3 variations), the credit is still consumed.

What's the difference between the extension and the web app?

They are functionally identical. The Chrome extension opens in a new tab when you click the icon. The web app runs directly in your browser at this website. Your account and credits work across both.
